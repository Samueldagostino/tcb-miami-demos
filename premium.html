Our Story

Born on the Block

What started as a dream and a grill became Westchester's worst-kept secret. The Chopping Block serves up smash burgers with crispy edges, hand-breaded fried chicken, and a house-made bacon jam that people can't stop talking about.

Every patty is smashed fresh to order. Every bun is toasted golden. Every bite is built to make you come back. We're not reinventing the burger — we're perfecting it.

The Man Behind the Grill
Meet Nick

Nick

Founder & Head Chef

Nick didn't go to culinary school. He didn't inherit a restaurant empire. What he had was an obsession with making the perfect smash burger — and the guts to park a truck on SW 87th Ave and prove it.

Every burger at The Chopping Block is hand-smashed with intention. "We take pride in not smashing our burgers to a crisp" — that philosophy defines everything. Crispy edges, juicy center, maximum flavor. The house-made bacon jam, the signature TCB sauce, the fresh Angus beef — every detail matters.

— Nick, The Chopping Block

The Journey
How We Got Here
2023

The First Smash

What started as a dream became reality when the truck pulled up on SW 87th Ave for the first time. The menu was simple: smash burgers, done right.

2024

Word Spreads

Google reviews started piling up — 4.9 stars. Regulars became evangelists. "Best burgers in Miami" stopped being a goal and became what people were saying.

2025

The Menu Grows

Fried chicken sandwiches. The legendary Chopped Cheese. Cajun fries. Loaded smash fries. The truck became a destination, not just a stop.

2026

The Block Keeps Growing

DoorDash. Uber Eats. Catering events. Private parties. What started as one truck is becoming a movement. And we're just getting started.
