Our Story
Meet the Team Behind the Block

What started as a passion for the perfect smash burger became The Chopping Block Miami — a food truck born in the heart of Westchester. Nick and the crew bring the heat every night, hand-smashing fresh Angus beef, crafting house-made bacon jam, and building burgers that have earned a 4.9-star reputation across 83 Google reviews. This isn't corporate fast food. This is street food with soul — made by people who actually care about every bite.

Nick — Founder & Head Chef

"We take pride in not smashing our burgers to a crisp." Every burger is smashed with intention — crispy edges, juicy center, maximum flavor.

Serving the Community

From our window to your hands — every order is made fresh and served with a smile. We're not just a truck, we're your neighborhood spot.
